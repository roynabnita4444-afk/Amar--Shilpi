A Symphony of Flavors & Memories

Located in the heart of Paikpara, North Kolkata, Amar Shilpi Restro Cafe is more than just a restaurant—it's a tribute to the legendary Kishore Kumar.

Step into a space adorned with vintage music imagery, monochrome wall art, and classic Bollywood references. Our air-conditioned dining space is designed to feel warm, cultural, and authentic to Kolkata.

  • Elegant & Inviting

    Perfect for family dining, luxurious meetups, and evening soirees.

  • Golden Era Charm

    Surrounded by monochrome wall art and classic Bollywood references.

  • Royal Service

    Experience hospitality reminiscent of the grand restaurants of old Calcutta.
